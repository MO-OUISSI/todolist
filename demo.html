üçÖ Pomodoro Technique

The Pomodoro Technique is a time management method developed by Francesco Cirillo:

  1. Focus for 25 minutes on a single task
  2. Take a 5-minute break
  3. Repeat 4 times
  4. Take a longer 15-30 minute break
